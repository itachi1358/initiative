About Us



50's School is a unique program designed for people aged 50 and above. It offers a mix of activities aimed at improving health, stimulating the mind, fostering social connections, and bringing back the fun of school days. Participants can look forward to classes on health maintenance, engaging workshops, recreational activities, and cultural programs, all in a supportive and vibrant community setting.

Our Vision

Our vision at 50's School is to transform retirement into a time of renewal, joy, and growth. We aim to create a lively and enriching environment where older adults can discover new passions, forge meaningful friendships, and maintain their health and well-being. By seamlessly combining education, recreation, and cultural experiences, we strive to provide our participants with the tools and opportunities to lead happy, active, and fulfilling lives. Our programs are designed to stimulate the mind, body, and spirit, ensuring that each individual can explore their interests, develop new skills, and enjoy a vibrant and dynamic lifestyle during their golden years.


Frequently Asked Questions

    Who is eligible to participate in the 50's School program?

    The 50's School program is specifically tailored for individuals aged 50 and above. It welcomes anyone within this age group who is looking to improve their health, engage in stimulating activities, and connect with others in a vibrant community setting.

    What types of activities and classes are offered at 50's School?

    Participants can look forward to a mix of activities, including classes on health maintenance, engaging workshops, recreational activities, and cultural programs. These activities are designed to provide a balanced experience that promotes physical well-being, mental stimulation, and social interaction.

    What is the vision of 50's School?

    The vision of 50's School is to transform retirement into a time of renewal, joy, and growth. The program aims to create a lively environment where older adults can discover new passions, make friends, and stay healthy. By combining education, recreation, and cultural experiences, 50's School strives to help participants lead happy, active, and fulfilling lives.
